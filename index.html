Our Story

More Than Just Coffee.
A Way of Life.

Founded in 2018 by two coffee-obsessed dreamers, Cafie was built on a single belief — that a truly great cup of coffee can change the course of your day. We source directly from sustainable farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala.

Every detail here is intentional — from the handpicked furniture to the playlist, from the water temperature to the grind size. We don't just serve coffee. We craft moments.
